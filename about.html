About Gator Tech Den

Your trusted local source for computer and game console repair and IT support in Waco, TX.

Our Story

Hi, I’m David, the owner and founder of Gator Tech Den. Technology has always been my passion, and I’ve spent years helping people repair, upgrade, and get the most out of their computers and devices.

Currently, Gator Tech Den is a growing local business that I operate independently. Many repairs are scheduled ahead of time so I can give each device the attention it deserves. My vision is to grow this into a full-time computer repair shop that Waco residents can rely on.

Our Values

At Gator Tech Den, we believe in clear communication, honest pricing, and quality service. Whether you’re a student, gamer, or small business owner, we treat every device like it’s our own. No hidden fees, no unnecessary upsells—just trustworthy repairs and IT support.

Why Choose Us?

We’re more than just a repair service. We’re your local tech partner in Waco, TX. From virus removal and upgrades to custom PC builds and data recovery, our goal is to keep your devices running smoothly so you can focus on what matters most.
